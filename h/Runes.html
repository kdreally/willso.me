Runes byþ one way of writ brooked bye þoe englesexes & oþer þeec folk like þoe Denc and Ducc before hye were gifen þæt Latin staffrow. For þe englesexes, þæt Latin staffrow was first shown to hem bye þoe Irishmen, and hye were made to adwesh runish writing by þoe Normans hoo cud ne read runes.

Þoe Runerows

Þere hafe been many runerows þrewute þoe þeec folkes stear. Þæt first was wat is clept "Elder Fuþark", wic came wense or þæt Old Hellenish or Old Italish staffrow was nimmed in by þoe Þeec folk. Þis was meant to make wiss þe eferic lude in heer tung cud be written eaþlic.

After þæt, þoe Anglish & Frisish folk wended it sum more to make Fuþorc, wic was still brooked by þoe Englesexes wen hie wrote Old English.
